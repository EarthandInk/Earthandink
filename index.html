Results-driven E-Commerce Merchandising Manager and Digital Brand Founder with 15+ years of experience bridging federal program management and direct-to-consumer brand building.

Founder of Earth & Ink — a newly launched quiet luxury lifestyle brand offering premium hand-poured coconut soy candles, original high-resolution digital art, and curated merchandise across Shopify and Etsy. Every product is conceived and produced entirely in-house, from the debut signature scent La Maison Des Roses — part of a candle range spanning 3 oz, 9.3 oz, and 16 oz across multiple fragrances — to the Cityscapes Reimagined collection and the Pop Art 2026 Limited Edition.

The role is front-end focused — storefront architecture, visual merchandising, product presentation, and brand identity. Not a developer or back-end engineer, but fluent in the tools and platforms that bring a brand to life at every customer touchpoint. Currently in an organic growth phase, building a loyal audience through consistent content and community before layering in paid strategy — with Google Analytics and paid campaign experience ready to deploy when the time is right.

Mockups are created in Canva and refined through Photopea — adjusting lighting, depth, and layering for polished, professional presentation. Fluent in emerging AI tools as part of an evolving creative workflow, bringing the same discerning eye and design sensibility to every medium. Video content is edited in CapCut across Instagram and YouTube. A natural and rapid adopter of new platforms — each one learned independently, with demonstrated results across Shopify, Etsy, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.
